Visit to Spain

Ibiza, Spain

Montserrat, Spain

Cuisine: Tapas

Ibiza, an island in Spain's Balearic archipelago, is renowned for its electrifying nightlife, pristine beaches, and vibrant atmosphere. It offers an unforgettable experience of music, relaxation, and stunning Mediterranean scenery.

Montserrat, near Barcelona, is famed for its unique rock formations, the Benedictine monastery Santa Maria de Montserrat, and panoramic views. Visitors enjoy hiking, exploring the monastery, and hearing the renowned Escolania boys' choir.

Tapas, small flavorful dishes, showcase Spain's culinary diversity. Enjoyed socially, they offer a taste of Spanish culture, from patatas bravas to jamón ibérico, often paired with wine or beer, fostering conviviality and exploration of flavors.
