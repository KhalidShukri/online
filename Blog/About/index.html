Most Visited Destination

Eiffel Tower

Location: Paris, France

The Eiffel Tower, an iconic symbol of Paris, France, attracts over 7 million visitors annually. Completed in 1889, it stands as a testament to engineering brilliance and French cultural heritage. Located in the Champ de Mars park, it offers panoramic views of the city and remains a must-see landmark for tourists worldwide.
